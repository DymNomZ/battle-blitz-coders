# ⚔️ Battle Blitz Coders 💻
*A 2D Java RPG Capstone Project by **BrosBeCoding***

Welcome to **Battle Blitz Coders**, an action-packed, story-driven 2D RPG made during our 2nd year, 1st semester as our capstone project! You must fight your way through slimes, viruses, and the ultimate test of all college students: **Sir Jay Vince Serato**!. 

The title screen greets you with the iconic **GLE Building**, playing a beautiful piano arrangement of **Maroon and Gold** by our very own team member, **Zillion**. 

As you progress through, you will fight slimes and viruses. But the true nightmare awaits in the _Final Stage_. Where Sir Serato himself will actively spawn **Peashooters** and **Squashes** to stop you from passing the course XD. 

You think you got what it takes? Try our game now!

## 🎮 Playable Characters
Choose your fighter from the class roster:
*   **DYMES** (That's me!)
*   **SETH**
*   **ZILLION**
*   **RAYMOND**

## 🎒 Arsenal & Inventory
Forget swords and shields; real coders fight with school supplies!

### 🔫 Ranged Weapons (Left-Click to shoot)
*   **Pencils & Ballpens:** Standard writing tools turned deadly projectile shooters.
*   **Paper Airplanes:** Folded to perfection for aerodynamic damage.

### 🔨 Melee Weapons (Left-Click to attack)
*   **Books:** Heavy textbooks full of knowledge and blunt force trauma.
*   **Sticks:** Picked up from the campus grounds.
*   **Breadboards & Calculators:** Hardware tools perfect for close-quarter combat.
*   **Nokia Phone:** Indestructible. Heavy. The ultimate weapon. 

### 🍕 Healing Items (Right-Click to consume)
*   Piattos
*   Bandaids
*   Pizza
*   Grab Food Box (The ultimate delivery of health)
* (There's even a legendary smelly sock in there somewhere, handle with care!)*

## ⌨️ Controls
*   **Move:** Standard Movement keys.
*   **Number Keys `1` - `5`:** Select item slot in the hotbar.
*   **`Left-Click`:** Attack / Shoot / Use weapon.
*   **`Right-Click`:** Consume healing item.
*   **`Q`:** Drop selected item.
*(Stuck? Look for the in-game NPC who will gladly explain all the mechanics to you!)*
